# Obsidian notes formatting

https://help.obsidian.md/How+to/Format+your+notes

## Basic formatting
- *italic* or _italic_
- **bold** or __bold__
- ==highlight text==
- ~~strikethrough~~
- Adding an inline footnote ^[Inline footnotes are easy].

## Citation block
